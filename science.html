Science

Audio ecology.

We use AudioMoth recorders and machine learning tools to monitor birds and bats across the woodland.

Recorders

AudioMoth devices

Small recorders are placed around the woodland to collect sound over set periods.

Analysis

Machine learning

ML tools help sort the recordings and identify likely bird calls and bat activity.

Birds

Bird activity

Bird records help show which areas are being used and how that changes through the year.

Bats

Bat activity

Bat recordings give another view of habitat use, especially around edges, paths, and clearings.
